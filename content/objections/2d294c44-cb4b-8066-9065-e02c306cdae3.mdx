<Toggle title="🇮🇹 Italy" level={2}>

- Dr. Paolo Manzo Study - Retrospective study:
  **11-month follow-up**

  Key outcomes:

  - **24.5% fewer total appointments**
  - **80.8% fewer unscheduled emergency visits**
  - **40.4% fewer hygiene visits**
  - **312% increase in hygiene instructions**
  → [[**View full study – Manzo et al., 2023**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1FTU7C5oFTvNIjaxCVBRBQchLLLN_0br8/view?usp=sharing)]

</Toggle>

<Toggle title="🇫🇷 France" level={2}>

<Callout emoji="💡">

Evidence

</Callout>

- **Dr. Jean Guilloux Case Study**
  Key outcomes:

  - **5 unnecessary emergency appointments avoided**
  - Effective **remote tie-loss management**
  - Improved hygiene compliance
  - Better patient experience and workflow efficiency
  → [[**View full study – Guilloux et al., 2024**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1sg8tny4o2_U4RcX14VV4oQfTIH5ndKpS/view)]


- **OrthoUP Study:** 12-month multicenter cohort – self-ligating braces
  Key outcomes:

  - **25% fewer total appointments**
  - **28% less chair time**
  - **32% lower no-show rate**
  - **26% better scheduling predictability**
  - **Consistently better MGI scores**
  → [[**View full study – OrthoUP Braces, 2024**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1wzqLUlaCiIO9xMRykwu-h1iC7IF4riYq/view)]

Evidence

- **Dr. Barry Benton case study** on AW passivity with SL braces (Damon)
  Key outcomes:

  - **Treatment completed in 20 months**
  - **Only 10 total in-office appointments**
  - **1 emergency visit**
  - **6 clinical incidents detected remotely**
  - **83 patient scans reviewed**
  - Improved clinical efficiency and patient compliance
  → [[**View full white paper – Benton, 2025**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1dqnTzhftk0x18DrxGpZ9BIn-H0Rp_oFO/view?usp=sharing)]


- **Dr. Jacquee Schieck white paper on LighForce + DM**
  Key outcomes:

  - **2.5× increase in net production**
  - **Value per visit more than doubled**
  - **~50% reduction in appointments per case** (21 → 12)
  - **Monitoring intervals extended up to 16 weeks**
  - Improved workflow intentionality and scalability
  → [[**View full white paper – Schieck, 2025**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1EtkzYq4EhGsyX10PRPeBqiKb20kbXZW8/view?usp=sharing)]
