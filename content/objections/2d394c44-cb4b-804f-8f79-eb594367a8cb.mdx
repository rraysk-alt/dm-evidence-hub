## **1. Carriere motion **

![](/images/objections/2d394c44-cb4b-804f-8f79-eb594367a8cb/image-7.png)

<Callout emoji="💡">

[**Dr. Mike Ragan’s Study**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1UAzZnAxRruBCjMNyOV_vbmHjkf9qPa5F/view?usp=sharing)

</Callout>

15 DM patients vs 15 Control:

- **Treatment Duration:** DM reduced duration by 25.21% (from 229.27 to 170.47 days).
- **In-Office Check Appointments:** Lowered from 2.93 to 0.6 per patient.
- **Average Value per Visit:** Increased to $1,215.25 (Control: $663.00).
- **Net Chairtime Cost Saving:** Averaged $252.33 per treatment with DM.
- The average total number of appointments was reduced by 45% with DM.
<Callout emoji="💡">

[**Dr. Monica Reinach's study**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Dkb4E37hOUeiYML2IOZ5cQ-bg6fDKWYM/view?usp=sharing)

</Callout>

**11 DM patients vs. 10 Non-DM:**

- The DM cohort had a 37% reduction in the total number of appointments (3.09 vs. 4.9).
- The DM cohort experienced a 24% decrease in treatment time (167.91 vs. 219.90 days).
- The incidence of treatment-related emergencies was reduced by almost 50%.
## **2. Rapid maxillary expander**

![](/images/objections/2d394c44-cb4b-804f-8f79-eb594367a8cb/image-8.png)

<Callout emoji="💡">

[**Dr. Mike Ragan’s Study**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1UAzZnAxRruBCjMNyOV_vbmHjkf9qPa5F/view?usp=sharing)

</Callout>

14 DM, 15 Control:

- **Treatment Duration:** DM shortened duration by 27.4% (from 167.47 to 121.6 days).
- **In-Office Check Appointments:** Reduced from 2.47 to 0.36 per patient.
- **Average Value per Visit:** Increased to $1,645.76 (Control: $869.40).
- **Net Chairtime Cost Saving:** Averaged $205.26 per treatment with DM.
- The DM group had a 47% decrease in the average total number of appointments.

<Callout emoji="💡">

[**University of Washington study**](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1AvIoIofB7kYDaA0SFxAkWdcmK1ivdLJW/view)

</Callout>

Patients had an average of **2.1** visits - The study's conclusion also states that in-person evaluation of maxillary expansion with a Hyrax expander can be replaced with remote monitoring using the DM software because the system was accurate enough in measuring intermolar width and flagging the correction of crossbite.
